About

The Computer Society of India is the largest body of computer professionals in India. We are a group of skilled designers, developers and tech enthusiasts who engage in a lot of projects and hackathons. To help push technology forward, we organise a wide range of workshops, conferences, events and competitions both technical and non-technical.

Events

Devspace

Hackathon

With an impressive prize pool, our flagship event, Devspace attracts over 1500 students every year. Devspace primarily consists of a hackathon with each track presenting various opportunities for students to apply their talents to solve real-world problems and provide innovative solutions. The event also features workshops and talks from professionals working in leading technical and business ventures, providing a hands-on learning experience for all participants.
